Week 1 Notes

How to Rewire Your Brain for Mobile First UX

Devices come in very different forms, we have to adapt websites to display correctly on all of them.

MOBILE FIRST!

How to Improve UX by Understanding Users

Find ways to fill your user's needs as simply as possible. A lot of times, people don't know what they want until you show it to them.

Three important thing to keep in mind:

  1. IMMEDIATE: Be there.
  2. RELEVANT: Be useful.
  3. FRICTIONLESS: Be quick.

Better Mobile UX with Strategic Menu Design

Primary controls! We have to make controls be intuitive and easy to reach. By putting the controls at the bottom on mobile, we improve reachability and conversion rates.

Designing Intuitive and Mobile Friendly Forms

Just ask the right information! Don't put unnecesary inputs, like job title when you're trying to make your user sign for a newsletter.

Another important thing with forms is to follow the standars when building them, this allow proper autocompletion, correct input types (also helps user see the right keyboard on mobile), etc.

Designing a Better Mobile Advertising User Experience

Users come to your website for content, give them what they want. We have to be really careful when placing ads, specially on mobile.

And remember: stop using pop-us!
